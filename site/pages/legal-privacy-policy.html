Privacy Policy

This policy describes how skillfoundry.work handles website and communication data. It applies to the public website and project communication channels, not to third-party services you use independently.

Data Minimization and Transparency

What We Collect

  • Basic access logs (IP, user agent, timestamp, requested resource) for security and uptime operations.
  • Voluntary messages you send through project communication channels (for example GitHub issues/discussions).
  • Operational telemetry required for abuse prevention, rate limiting, and availability monitoring.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No sale of personal data.
  • No intentional collection of sensitive personal categories through the public website.
  • No persistent tracking beyond what is required for operations unless explicitly announced.

Use, Retention, and Security

  • Data is used to operate, secure, and improve website and project support workflows.
  • Log retention is limited to the minimum practical period for security and troubleshooting.
  • Access to operational data is restricted to authorized maintainers.
  • If a data incident occurs, impacted users will be informed according to applicable requirements.

Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we control, subject to legal obligations.
  • You may request clarification about processing purpose and retention period.
